About Me

Aman Tiwari is a technically proficient computer science student specializing in cybersecurity with a strong foundation in both software development and network security. Proficient in multiple programming languages, including C++, C, Java, and Python, he has developed a variety of projects showcasing his technical abilities. His expertise extends to web development using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, along with front-end frameworks like React. Aman has hands-on experience in cloud computing with Azure, and he is well-versed in network administration and security, including cryptography and router configuration. His ability to work with data structures and operating systems further strengthens his technical profile. Additionally, Aman is skilled in using version control systems like Git and GitHub, facilitating effective project collaboration and code management. Aman’s technical acumen is complemented by his leadership in organizing and leading technical workshops, hackathons, and community initiatives, demonstrating not only his knowledge but also his ability to apply it in practical, real-world scenarios.
